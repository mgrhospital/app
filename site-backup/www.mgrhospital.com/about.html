About the Doctor


Dr M Ganeswara Rao has completed his MBBS Degree from Andhra University, Andhra Medical College and MS General Surgery from and Rangaraya Medical College.

- He worked as the Head of the Department of General Surgery at King George Hospital and as a Professor for the 1st Unit in Andhra Medical College.
- Undergone training in Laproscopic Surgery at Apollo Hosital, Hyderabad under the guidance of Dr Ravindranadh, Senior member of the Association of Surgeons of India.
