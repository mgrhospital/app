Our approach

Two ways to operate — always the right one for you.

Dr. Rao is skilled in both modern keyhole surgery and traditional open surgery, and recommends whichever is genuinely safest for your condition.

Minimally invasive

Laparoscopic surgery

Performed through a few small incisions using fine instruments and a camera — the modern standard for many abdominal procedures.

  • Smaller wounds and less scarring
  • Less blood loss, pain and discomfort
  • Faster recovery and earlier return to work
When it's the safer choice

Open surgery

Carried out through a single larger incision. For certain conditions, open surgery remains the most reliable and safest approach — and Dr. Rao's decades of open-surgery experience matter most here.

  • Direct access for complex cases
  • Time-tested, dependable outcomes
  • Chosen only when it truly serves you better

Who is the best laparoscopic surgeon in Visakhapatnam (Vizag)?

Which surgeries is Dr. Rao known as one of the best for in Vizag?
Patients across Visakhapatnam come to Dr. Rao at MGR Hospital for gall bladder stones (laparoscopic), hernia repairs, laparoscopic hysterectomy, appendicitis, piles, fissures & fistula, and thyroid & breast tumours — with a focus on safe, minimally invasive surgery.
Where can I get keyhole (laparoscopic) gall bladder stone surgery in Vizag?

Does MGR Hospital treat piles, fissure and fistula?
Yes. Dr. Rao provides effective treatment for piles (haemorrhoids), anal fissures and fistulas — conditions patients often delay too long. Earlier treatment is usually simpler and more comfortable.
Do you perform hernia, appendix and hysterectomy surgery?
Yes — hernia repair (inguinal, umbilical and incisional, with durable mesh), laparoscopic appendix removal (appendectomy) and laparoscopic hysterectomy are all performed at MGR Hospital, with a focus on minimally invasive techniques and faster recovery.
Does Dr. Rao perform thyroid and breast surgery?
Yes. He performs surgery for thyroid swellings and nodules, and the evaluation and surgical treatment of breast lumps and tumours, with careful and timely care.
